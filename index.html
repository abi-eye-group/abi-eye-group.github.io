Tips

The demonstrations should work fine in Chrome. You may notice motion artefacts (e.g., comet tails) which are typical for LED monitors. You may want to reduce these effects you may wish to consult Blur Busters for more information. One option is nVidia's Ultra Low Motion Blur (ULMB) technology for motion blur reduction. ULMB2 is the latest release. We have also used a 120Hz AMOLED Tablet (Samsung Galaxy Tab S8 Ultra) which works as well. If you want to scale your display correctly you will need to fill in "Display Options". You can get these details here
